Max Pooling

Max pooling is a downsampling technique used in Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to reduce the spatial dimensions of feature maps while retaining the most important information. It works by dividing the input into non-overlapping rectangular regions and selecting the maximum value from each region.
